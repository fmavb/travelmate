About TravelMate

Travelmate is an online group which you could share you own travel experience, make your travel passport, as well as see beautiful views and experiences from other members around the world.

Online Passport

As our special Travelmate members, you are able to customise your own special travel passport online by adding as many trips as you want, your passport would be sorted by country and we would finally give you the proportion of the world that you travelled!

You could also see your trips on a map, by lines that shows your destinations around the world.

See Other member's trips

You are able to load the most popular trips and most recent trips thtough our website members which as we believe would give you some suggestions for your next travel experience.

Be the top traveller

Be the top traveller means that you are really a real professional traveller with the most trips, you would be able to see the top ranking travel members list through our website, as well as the most active travellers.

Your Personal Profile

You could also see your own recent trips in your own profile where you could also add your trip blog and pictres.

For any other questions, you could always contact us, we would be very happy to help you.
