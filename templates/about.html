About TravelMate

Travelmate is an online community which enables you to share your own travel experience, make your travel passport, as well as see beautiful views and experiences of other members around the world.

Online Passport

As our special Travelmate member, you are able to customise your own special travel passport online by adding as many trips as you want, your passport would be sorted by country and we would finally give you the proportion of the world that you travelled!

You can also see your trips on a map, by lines that shows your destinations around the world.

See Other members' trips

You are able to load the most popular trips and most recent trips through our website members which as we believe would give you some suggestions for your next travel experience.

Be the top traveller

Be the top traveller means that you are a real professional traveller with the most trips, you will be able to see the top ranking travel members list through our website, as well as the most active travellers.
